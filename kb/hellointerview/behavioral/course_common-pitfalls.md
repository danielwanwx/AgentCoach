It's hard to make a firm statement here. Interviewers react variably to the candidate seeming over-rehearsed. So a well-integrated "let me of which situation I want to share here" might land better than a suspicious glance at a notepad followed.

You're emphasizing "small keyword notes" and I think that's the right ticket. If you're going to use them, treat them as memory prompts rather than a script to follow.
