I had the opportunity of doing the open ended interview some weeks ago and you are going with your setup, they do not provide anything for you except what you are suppose to build and incrementally adding features and edge cases depending on how fast you build and how much time you have left,

In my own case i was asked to build the frontend of a sheduling system like calendly where a student can see the  availability of a tutor and book that tutor. Different constraints and edge cases were introduced later in the build like having multiple tutors with multiple students, students offering different subjects and tutors offering different subjects and this intersection will determine the availability different students see at different times.

You also get to discuss different trade offs and why you are choosing an approach as you build with different scalability issues. The whole section was about 90 mins
