You have a lot of different people who are expecting you to ask dumb questions at the start, so I wouldn't overstress about this. But do spread the load! Even if you get assigned a "ramp-up buddy", make a deliberate attempt to go ask other people questions.

In general notetaking is very personal, so aside from "it seems like a good idea!" I'm not sure I'd making sweeping generalizations here. I usually keep a scratch pad of open threads (things I don't know, people I haven't met, etc), answer those questions inline and add new questions to the bottom. Then when I meet someone new I can scan through those questions so I always have something reasonably detailed to get to.
