I recently had an interview with them a week ago. The questions they ask you aren't very straightforward. They bundle questions from leetcode and apply it to a whole new scenario. Their coding questions are divided into 3 parts where the latter questions rely on the preceding ones. For example, I was asked a question that was the closest to Flood fill for the first question and Rotten orange for the second question. Each question is 1 page long. Good luck to everybody who's having an interview with them.

PS. I don't know what the 3rd part was. I almost completed the 2nd part. My advice is to practice the questions listed above and have a solid knowledge in DSA. Since I haven't done DSA as much lately, I took more time then I should have.
