I just want to share my experience with the Product Architecture interview. I did a full E5 loop 4 years ago and a M1 Screen last month and got the "Facebook Feed" question both times. I both cases they just wanted to focus on the UI and API , explicitly stating that they were not looking for any high level design and that we will not be drawing any diagrams! I truly felt unprepared both times as I was focusing on High level\low level system design in my prep, but passed the interview both times to my surprise probably due to having prior experience designing and building APIs....

My advise to those taking the PA interview is to be comfortable with API design , when to use cursors vs pagination , pre fetching , what should be in the response , API security etc...
