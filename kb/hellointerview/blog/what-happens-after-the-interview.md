Many smaller companies have the hiring manager own the process. They are usually eager to fill seats and rarely will they stack up several qualified candidates over more than a couple weeks before making a decision. Most companies try to govern the parallelism of the process by deciding how many people they let in at the top.

Very hard to generalize across all companies though, the interview types aren’t as important as the size/stage of the company and the maturity of their recruiting processes.
